Key Features in Detail

GPT-4o — The All-in-One Model

GPT-4o (released May 2024) unified all of OpenAI's capabilities into one model. Text, images, audio, and soon video — all processed by the same AI. The result: faster responses, better understanding of images, and the ability to "see" what you're showing it. Unlike its predecessors, GPT-4o can look at a photo, read a menu, understand a meme, and even read emotions from facial expressions in real time.

DALL-E — Image Generation Built In

No need to leave ChatGPT to generate images. Ask "create an image of..." and DALL-E generates 4 options. You can then refine, edit parts of the image, or expand the canvas. In 2026, DALL-E integration is seamless — ChatGPT understands when you're describing something that needs visual creation.

Advanced Data Analysis (Code Interpreter)

Upload any data file (CSV, Excel, PDF, JSON) and ChatGPT writes Python code to analyze it. It creates charts, runs statistical tests, merges datasets, and even builds simple web apps. In 2026, this is the single most underrated feature — many data analysts use ChatGPT as their daily analysis tool.

GPT Store & Custom GPTs

Create your own ChatGPT with custom instructions, knowledge files, and specific capabilities. Thousands of GPTs are available in the GPT Store for everything from "SEO Assistant" to "Fitness Coach" to "Travel Planner."

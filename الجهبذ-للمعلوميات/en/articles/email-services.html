Google Workspace — The Gold Standard

Google Workspace is the most popular choice for businesses. Gmail is known for its superior search capabilities and spam protection. Google Docs, Sheets, Meet, and Drive — the integration between them creates a complete work environment. If your team already uses Google, this is the natural choice.

Google Workspace — $6/month

Professional email + Google Meet + Drive + Docs — the all-in-one business platform.

workspace.google.com

Zoho Mail — Best Value for Small Businesses

Zoho offers a generous free plan (up to 5 users) with 5GB storage per user — ideal for startups. Paid plans start from just €1/month with 50GB storage. Zoho Workplace — an integrated toolset including Writer, Sheet, Show, and Cliq. The most economical choice with respectable performance.

Zoho Mail — Start Free

Free professional email for up to 5 users. Paid plans from €1/month — best value for small businesses.

zoho.com/mail

Microsoft 365 — For Companies Living in the Office World

Word, Excel, and PowerPoint — if your team works on them daily, Microsoft 365 is the logical choice. Outlook is known for its strong organizational capabilities in email. Teams has become the primary communication tool for businesses. Bonus: 50GB storage in the basic plan.

Microsoft 365 — $6/month

Outlook + Word + Excel + Teams + OneDrive — the classic powerful business environment.

microsoft.com/365

Final Verdict

Google Workspace: The most comprehensive choice — for a team already using Google
Zoho Mail: Best for limited budgets — powerful free plan for startups
Microsoft 365: Classic power — for companies relying on Office

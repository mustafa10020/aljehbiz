What Makes Claude Stand Out

200K Token Context Window

Claude can process entire novels, hundreds of pages of code, or hours of meeting transcripts in a single prompt. Upload 3-5 books, ask Claude to find specific themes, and it will. This massive context window is the largest among major AI models, making Claude the go-to for document analysis, legal review, and long-form research.

Artifacts — Real-Time Collaboration

When you ask Claude to write code, create a design, or draft a document, it appears in an Artifact — a separate, editable window. You can edit the code directly and Claude sees your changes. This back-and-forth creates a collaborative coding experience unlike any other AI tool. For developers, Artifacts alone justify the Pro subscription.

Projects — Organized Knowledge

Claude Projects let you upload reference documents, set custom instructions, and maintain context across conversations. A marketing team can upload their brand guidelines, tone of voice document, and past campaign data — every conversation in that Project will reference these materials automatically.

🚀 Growth Strategy

  • Week 1-2: Post 1 article/day (rotate AR/EN/ES). Share in relevant Facebook groups (Tech, SEO, Travel, Entrepreneurship)
  • Week 3-4: Invite friends, run a small ad campaign ($3-5/day) targeting Saudi Arabia, Egypt, UAE for Arabic; US/UK/India for English; Mexico/Spain/Argentina for Spanish
  • Month 2: Create a "Al-Jehabiz" character video series (3-minute quick reviews). Use the character as a mascot in all visuals
  • Month 3: Cross-post to LinkedIn (English), Twitter/X (English + Arabic threads), Pinterest (infographics), and Telegram (Arabic channel)
  • Monetization: After 1,000 followers, start pinning affiliate links in comments. Create "exclusive" Facebook-only affiliate offers
